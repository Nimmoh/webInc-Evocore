Our Background
About Us

Evocore Tech was founded with a clear-eyed understanding of the challenges Kenyan SMEs face when it comes to technology, solutions that are either too generic, too expensive, or both. We set out to change that.

Our team brings together developers, designers, strategists, and support specialists who have spent their careers in this market. We don't parachute in best practices from elsewhere; we build from the ground up with local context in mind.

From day one, our commitment has been simple: don't just deliver software, become a partner your business can rely on, year after year.
